Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about CourtesyChain, partnerships, and technical support.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical support, our team aims to respond within 4-6 hours. Partnership and investment inquiries receive priority scheduling.

I'm interested in partnering. What's the process?

Send us an email at partnerships@courtesychain.io with details about your organization and proposed collaboration. Our team will schedule a discovery call within 3-5 business days to discuss potential synergies and next steps.
